Nombre del proyecto

Do you have the right stuff?

We will never stop pushing the limits of design, architecture and the capabilities of the platforms we embrace. Our future promises continued growth of both our capacity and capabilities in all areas of our business. And that’s where you come in. If you choose to get to know Bottle Rocket better, you’ll learn that we are a values-driven business where things like “embrace the impossible,” “take your craft personally” and “serve the user” really mean something. Everyday, you’ll see passionate Rocketeers debating design issues at whiteboards on nearly every wall, focusing on tough problems in complex code, leading client meetings with the world’s

top brands, conducting user testing to find areas to improve our work and creating project plans to release the next award-winning app. If you interview with Bottle Rocket, you’ll meet passionate people who ask tough questions and, while they won’t expect you to know every answer, they will expect you know how to figure it out by thinking on your feet and using your resources wisely. There’s going to be a lot of interviews, probably some testing and scenario-based questioning as well. It’s not easy to get a job at Bottle Rocket, so please don’t apply if you’re not seriously passionate about building the best possible experiences on mobile.
